# Player Guide (very short)

## Goal
Follow your matches live and receive target calls.

## Get started
- Open the app.
- Go to `Notifications` and sign in.
- Allow browser notifications.

## During the tournament
- Check `Live` to see pools, standings, and matches.
- When a notification arrives, go to the assigned target.
- Progression depends on ranking: elimination, another pool stage, or a bracket.

## Tips
- Keep the `Notifications` page open when possible.
- Make sure notifications are not blocked by the browser.
